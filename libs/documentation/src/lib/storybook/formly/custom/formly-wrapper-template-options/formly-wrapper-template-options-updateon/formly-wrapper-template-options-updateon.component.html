By default, the updateOn option assigned to a field will be passed to any sub-field contained in fieldGroup. In this example, blur is assigned to the top field for it's updateOn option. As a result, the input fields contained in it's fieldGroup which do not assign their own updateOn option will also update on blur. However, by specifying a different updateOn choice, a field can be made to update differently than its ancestors.
